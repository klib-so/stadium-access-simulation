\section{Summery Findings}\label{sec:summery-findings}
The simulation was run through three iterations with the following fixed parameters:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Tickets sold: 5000
    \item Entry Points: 1
    \item Initial Population: 0
    \item Simulation Start Time: 17:00
    \item Simulation End Time: 19:20
    \item Game Kickoff Time: 19:00
    \item Service Time Distribution
    \item \begin{itemize}
              \item Type: Shifted Exponential
              \item Minimum Service Time: 3 seconds
              \item Service Rate (lambda): 0.61
    \end{itemize}
    \item Arrival Distribution
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Type: Normal
        \item Free Variable: Time
        \item Mean: 18:23 (37 minutes before kickoff)
        \item Standard deviation: 9.2
    \end{itemize}
\end{itemize}
    During each iteration the following data was collected on each spectator:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Arrival Time
    \item Processing Time (time taken passing through the turnstile)
    \item Admission Time
    \item Total Time (total time taken from arrival to being seated)
\end{itemize}
    The data was collected in CSV format for statistical analysis.
\subsection{First Iteration (6 turnstiles)}
    The first simulation was run with six turnstiles in operation.
    It resulted in 88\% of ticket holders being seated before the kickoff time, with an average wait time of 14 minutes
    and 6 seconds, and a maximum wait time of 28 minutes and 42 seconds.

    \subsection{Second Iteration (7 turnstiles)}
    The second simulation was run with seven turnstiles in operation.
    It resulted in 98\% of spectators being seated at kickoff time, with an average wait time of 10 minutes and 19
    seconds, and a maximum wait time of 21 minutes and 5 seconds.

    \subsection{Third Iteration (8 turnstiles)}
    The third simulation was run with eight turnstiles in operation.
    It resulted in 100\% of spectators being seated at kickoff time, with an average wait time of 20 minutes and 10
    seconds, and a maximum wait time of 15 minutes and 36 seconds.
